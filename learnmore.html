About Our Company

RARE MODELS is a boutique model management company based in Ghana, representing both female and male models. Founded in 2012 by Solomon Senanu, with extensive experience in men's fashion, the company offers a personalized, professional service to clients and models alike.

Our booking team, led by Kwaku Holiday, known for his appearance on Channel 4's "The Model Agency," has in-depth knowledge of international markets and speaks multiple languages. We pride ourselves on a friendly, courteous service while nurturing models from development stages to career management.

Our Services

  • Scouting and grooming of both male and female models
  • Personalized career management and development
  • Booking for fashion shows, editorial shoots, and advertising campaigns
  • International talent representation
  • Professional portfolio creation and enhancement
